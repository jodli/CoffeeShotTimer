Privacy Policy for Coffee Shot Timer

Last Updated: July 2025

Information We Do NOT Collect

Coffee Shot Timer is completely offline. We do NOT:

  • Collect personal data (name, email, location)
  • Track usage or analytics
  • Require account creation
  • Connect to the internet

Local Data Storage

All data (shots, beans, settings) is stored locally on your device only. Never transmitted anywhere.
