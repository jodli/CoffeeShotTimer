Why Coffee Shot Timer?

Everything you need to perfect your espresso extractions in one beautiful, offline-first app.

Precision Timing

Track your extraction time with a large, easy-to-read timer designed for use in the kitchen.

  • Clear time display
  • Start/stop controls
  • Easy to use interface

Smart Tracking

Log your shots with detailed information including timing, ratios, and grinder settings.

  • Shot history logging
  • Bean management
  • Grinder settings tracking

100% Offline & Dark Mode

No internet required. Your data stays on your device. Beautiful dark and light themes for any time of day.

  • No tracking or accounts
  • Local data only
  • Light & dark themes

Precision Shot Timer with Weight Tracking

Track your extraction time with a visual progress ring that shows when you're in the optimal zone. Record both input and output weights with intuitive sliders for perfect brew ratios.

  • Visual timer with optimal zone indicator
  • Weight in/out tracking (18g → 36g)
  • Visual weight sliders for easy input
  • Selected bean display with freshness

Smart Bean Management with Photos

Organize your coffee collection with package photos and automatic freshness tracking. See at a glance which beans are fresh and track your last grind settings for each.

  • Bean package photo capture
  • Automatic freshness indicators
  • Last grind setting per bean
  • Tasting notes and descriptions

Advanced Shot Analytics

Get detailed insights with statistics on extraction times, brew ratios, and trends. Identify your most-used grinder settings and see distribution charts of your shot quality.

  • Overall statistics dashboard
  • 30-day trend analysis
  • Brew ratio distribution charts
  • Extraction time analysis
